RESEARCH

My work centres on radio observations of neutron stars and fast radio transients. Using some of the world's largest telescopes, such as the Arecibo Observatory in Puerto Rico and Green Bank Observatory in West Virginia, I develop software to search for new pulsars and Fast Radio Bursts, and study their properties.
